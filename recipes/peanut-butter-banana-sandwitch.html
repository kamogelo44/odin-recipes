Peanut Butter And Banana Sandwitch

Home

Description

This is Recepe to make peanut butter sandwitch. Peanutbutter banana sandwitch is a quick and easy to make snack. If you are looking to make something tasty and quick then you have arrived to the right recepe.

Ingredients

  • 1 Banana.
  • 2 slices of bread (white or brown).
  • Peanut butter.
  1. Spread peanut butter on both sandwitches.
  2. Peel of the banna skin, then chop you banana into mutliple slices.
  3. Place the slices on top of one slice of bread in any order.
  4. Add the slice the other slice on top of the bread with the the banana slices and enjoy.

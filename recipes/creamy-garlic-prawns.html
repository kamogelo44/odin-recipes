10-Minut Creamy Garlic Prawns

Home

Description

A simple, restaurant-quality meal made in one pan.

Ingredients

  • Prawns.
  • Garlic.
  • cream (optional: parsley, white wine).
  1. Sauté garlic in butter.
  2. add prawns, cook for 1–2 minutes.
  3. Add cream.
  4. Simmer for a few minutes until thickened, and serve with parsley.

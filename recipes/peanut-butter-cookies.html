Peanut Butter Cookies

Home

Description

A fast, gluten-free, and simple sweet treat.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter.
  • Sugar.
  • 1 egg.
  1. Mix 1 cup peanut butter.
  2. 1/2 cup sugar, and 1 egg
  3. Form into balls and flatten with a fork.
  4. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 10–12 minutes. Leave to cool then enjoy.
